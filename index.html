Our Story

About Payel Food Products

Founded with a passion for authentic Bengali flavors, Payel Food Products has been serving premium quality snacks since 2020. Our journey began with a simple mission — to bring the traditional taste of homemade snacks to every household.

From our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility, we produce over 50+ varieties of snacks including chanachur, bhujia, mixture, namkeen, and more. Every product is crafted with love, premium ingredients, and strict quality control measures.
