About Payel Food Products

Premium snacks made with love and tradition since 2020

Our Story

Founded in 2020 in Kolkata, Payel Food Products started with a dream — to bring the authentic taste of traditional Bengali snacks to every household across India. What began as a small family venture has grown into one of the most trusted food brands in the region.

Our founder's passion for quality and authentic flavors drives every product we make. We source only the finest ingredients, follow strict quality protocols, and pack everything with care to ensure freshness reaches your doorstep.

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide premium, hygienic, and affordable food products that preserve Bengal's rich culinary heritage.

Vision: To become India's most trusted premium snack brand known for authentic flavors and sustainable practices.
