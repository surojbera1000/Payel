About Payel Food Products

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Payel Food Products started with a simple dream — to bring the authentic taste of traditional Bengali snacks to every household across India. What began as a small family venture in Kolkata has now grown into one of the most trusted food brands in the region.

Our founder's passion for quality and taste has been the driving force behind every product we make. We believe that great food brings people together, and every packet of Payel Food is crafted with that belief.

Our Mission

To provide premium quality, hygienic, and affordable food products that preserve the rich culinary heritage of Bengal while meeting modern standards of food safety and taste.

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted premium snack brand, known for authentic flavors, innovative products, and sustainable manufacturing practices.

Manufacturing Excellence

Our state-of-the-art manufacturing facility is equipped with modern machinery and follows international food safety standards. Every product goes through rigorous quality checks before reaching you.
